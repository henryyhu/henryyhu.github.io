Overview

In this course, we will focus on the study of theory, techniques, and applications of statistical methods for business analytics, particularly methods of regression, multivariate statistical analysis, and time series analysis. We will cover several important topics in data analysis including: data clearning, logistic regression, random forest, boosting and so on. We will also provide opportunities to gain hands-on experience of handling large-scale data set.
